You need a modern browser with javascript enabled to play this game.

#TweetWars

Program your strategy in a tweet. and stuff.

There are no rules yet. I'm looking for ideas.


## Physics
Field radius: 1 metre
Player mass: 2 Kg
Player radius: 6 cm
Force: [evaluation of strategy expresssion] Newtons

### Engine
Integrator: Runge Kutta
Collisions: fully elastic


## Forces you control:
r<  push toward centre. The yellow line is currently proportional to the evaluation of this expression.
  more coming soon...


## Variables available to you:
r  your distance from the centre
r' your speed toward the centre
r| your speed tangential to the center


## Math operators available to you (in order of precedence):
^   raise to power
* /  multiply, divide
+ -  add, subtract
< >  less-than, greater-than (evaluate to 1.0 or 0.0 for true/false)


## Math Bugs:
float literals  ".2" evaluates to 2. The lexer is eating leading decimals. Workaround: zero prefix — "0.2".

## Missing Math:
Brackets  Parentheses are currently a syntax error. You cannot control order of operations (yet!).
Unary minus  "-5" is currently a syntax error. Use 0-5.
Convenient Constants  e, pi, etc coming soon.
Absolute value  Not sure how I want this too look yet.
